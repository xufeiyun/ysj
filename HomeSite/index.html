See the information you care about.

To do your job well, you need the right information at the right time. Unfortunately, that information lives in an ever-increasing mess of disconnected spreadsheets, systems, databases and applications.

YinData solves that problem by bringing your business and its data together in one intuitive platform. With YinData, it’s easy to see all the information you care about in one place and use it to make faster, better-informed decisions.

Connect to any source of data.

  • spreadsheets

  • cloud-based apps

  • on premise

  • data warehouses

  • databases

  • social media

Take a moment to think about the data you rely on. Now think about where it comes from. What if data from all those sources was automatically pulled into one location, in real time? YinData makes that a reality. With YinData, you can quickly and easily connect to any source of data, no matter what it is or where it lives.

Build your dream dashboard.

YinData makes it easy to see the information you want, how and when you want it. Anyone—from CEO to contributor—has the ability to discover, mash up, visualize and present data any way they’d like. So whether you’re wanting to see marketing’s contribution to sales or manufacturing’s labor cost per unit, the information you need is always at your fingertips.

Stay connected anywhere you go.

Opportunities don’t wait for you to get back to your computer, so YinData helps you seize them on the go. With a seamless experience on any device, YinData delivers the information you want, wherever you happen to be.

Get everyone on the same page.

YinData is an organization-wide platform capable of aligning your company, its conversations and its actions around business objectives that deliver real business results. And it transforms the way you manage business.
